### Remarks

There are two types of PrecisionModel: Exact [`PrecisionModel`](../precisionmodel) (all digits are significant);Rounded [`PrecisionModel`](../precisionmodel) (some number of digits are significant). A [`PrecisionModel`](../precisionmodel) can be set to [`VectorLayer`](../vectorlayer) via [`DriverOptions`](../driveroptions) in order to round coordinates when writing or reading geometries.
